Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Pasta:
    • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti or fettuccine
  • Sauce:
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 large egg yolks
    • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Other Ingredients:
    • 150g (5 oz) pancetta or guanciale, diced
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • Salt (for pasta water)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pasta:
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Cook the Pancetta:
    1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add the diced pancetta or guanciale and cook until crispy. Remove the skillet from heat.
  4. Combine:
    1. Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the pancetta and toss to coat in the fat.
    2. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing constantly to create a creamy sauce. Use reserved pasta water, a few tablespoons at a time, to adjust the consistency as needed.
  5. Serve:

    Serve immediately, topped with extra grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.
