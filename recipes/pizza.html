Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • ¾ cup warm water (around 110°F/45°C)
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 cup canned tomato sauce
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toppings (Customize as You Like!):
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Pepperoni slices
    • Vegetables: mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives
    • Cooked chicken, sausage, or bacon
    • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the Dough:
    1. In a small bowl, combine yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
    3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    4. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:

    Mix tomato sauce, garlic powder, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  3. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.

  4. Shape the Dough:
    1. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    2. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan, baking sheet, or pizza peel (if using a stone).
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    1. Spread the prepared sauce evenly over the dough.
    2. Sprinkle cheese generously over the sauce.
    3. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake:

    Bake for 10–15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.

  7. Serve:

    Slice and serve your pizza hot with your favorite dips like ranch, garlic butter, or marinara.
